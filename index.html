Where Imagination Becomes Reality.

Alien innovations is a young innovators start up to make the lives of visually impaired people more comfortable and safe with our inventions. The BLIND EYE is our invention which helps the visually impaired people in 3 sides obstacle free navigation, reads the text and recognize the people for the visually impaired.
